author=Yasemin Usta; Xuan Zhou; Guglielmina Mutani
email=yasemin.usta@polito.it; xuan.zhou@polito.it; guglielmina.mutani@polito.it

about=This plugin computes hourly building- and climate-specific Air Change Rates (ACR) to improve infiltration modelling in Urban Building Energy Modelling (UBEM). It integrates wind speed corrections at facade level using urban canyon algorithms, outdoor temperature correction via the Urban Weather Generator (UWG/UMEP), and hourly ACR simulation using a Three-Zone Lumped Parameter Model (3ZLPM) or CONTAM. Inputs include building geometry, air permeability, roughness length, and hourly weather data.
